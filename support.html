How do I create a team?

Tap the Teams tab, then tap "Create Team". Choose a template or start from scratch. Share the invite code with your friends!

How does settlement work?

After your event, go to the Settlement view. Stashy automatically calculates who owes whom. You can use PayPal.me or Revolut to settle directly.

Can I use different currencies?

Yes! Stashy supports 20+ currencies including EUR, USD, GBP, CHF, and more. You can set a team currency and add expenses in any currency.

What's included in Pro?

Pro includes unlimited teams, members and expenses, receipt scanner, recurring expenses, and priority support.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Stashy Pro ‚Üí Restore Purchases. Your subscription will be restored automatically.

Is my data secure?

Yes! Stashy uses Apple's CloudKit for sync, which means your data is stored in your private iCloud container. We don't have access to your data.
